Weekly Meetings

Every Sunday, the HSA meets in Old Union on the 3rd floor for their weekly meeting. We begin with meditation, after which we delve into some bhajans (with instruments such as the harmonium and manjira), mantra discussions, and Aarti. Please join us one of these Sundays for our weekly meeting, and feel free to bring a bhajan or mantra to share with us too!
Sundays @ 7:30 P.M.
