About Us
The Stanford Hindu Students Association (HSA) is a VSO dedicated to bringing together students interested in learning about Sanatan Dharma and allowing them to meet like-minded individuals while connecting with their religion and culture. As a part of the HSA, students become part of a close-knit community where best friends are made. Together, this HSA community organizes events such as Diwali, plans trips to various places such as temples and other universities, partners with other on-campus organizations to hold massive community service events, and even goes on retreats to places like Lake Tahoe and Olema. We hope you are as excited as us to join HSA—not only to attend our events, but to help organize them as well!
