Diwali Information




Every fall, the entire HSA team comes together to put on one of the biggest on-campus South Asian festivals of the year! After months of preparation, we put on a massive event, with audiences not just from Stanford, but all around the Bay area! We have performances, speakers, and even food!
This year, Stanford Diwali will be held on Sunday, November 12, from 4 pm to 7:30 pm at the Memorial Church on Stanford campus. We will start off with a puja, where all students can join us on stage, after which we will move on to have performances from Stanfordâ€™s competitive dance and a cappella teams. For the first time ever, we will also be having teams from UC Berkeley performing with us as well! The program also includes student speakers, a guest speaker (to be announced!), as well as a massive Rangoli and even food!

Tickets for Stanford affiliates (with valid Stanford ID): Free
Tickets for non-Stanford students (with valid student ID): $5
Tickets for non-Stanford affiliates and non-students: $10
