Stanford Berkeley MeetUps

Once every few months, the Stanford HSA and Berkeley HSA meet up at a beautiful and calming location to make new friends and discuss various topics related to Sanatan Dharma. These meetups prove to be fruitful for both organizations due to the increased sense of community between the two clubs as well as the transfer of information that helps educate everyone in attendance about different aspects of Hinduism.
